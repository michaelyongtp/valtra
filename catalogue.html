About

First stablished in Singapore in 1994 as a supplier of high quality commercial and hospitality contract loose furniture, Valtra Galleria expanded into Malaysia (Valtra Furniture Designs Sdn Bhd) in 1999 eventually becoming one of the leading contract loose furniture suppliers in KL.

With a wide selection of furniture product lines imported from Europe and Asia, we provide professional furniture selection and supply services to a spectrum of commercial and hospitality institutions which includes but is not limited to interior design firms, hotels, schools as well as office spaces.

We also customise high good quality loose furniture to your desired specification and finishes.
